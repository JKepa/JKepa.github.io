Bio


Jowita Kepa is a visual artist; lives and works in Berlin; has exhibited throughout North America and Europe; producing visual and sound compositions, often using found footage and appropriation, confronting the viewer with a physical experience by either pushing the medium to an extreme or creating an external sensory overload, in an attempt to create a new visual language; has been supported by the Canada Council for the Arts, the Stiftung Kunstfonds in Germany and The Department of Culture and National Heritage, Kraków.
